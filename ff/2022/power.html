Week 2

From the desk of the Tom Brady, Porn Addict

Ah, week 1. Reactions abound. Some teams look invincible, others look... vincible.

Some quick notes from around the league:

  • Russel Wilson appears to be entirely too Christian. Folks, we've never seen this before-- a prodigiously bad suit, a cringeworthy offseason, and a return home only to lose to your hapless former team. I must admit I love the look of Jerry Jeu (Zou Bi-Jeu Bi-Jeuuu). But could this team once again finish last in their division?
  • The Cowboys. Yikes. Nico and Nader are already scheming obsessively to unload their DAL assets, and who can blame them? Cue A Cee-Dee C: they've both been Thunderstruck by the QB injury bug. Luckily, Dak is the only dearly QB departed this week (lest we forget #1 MILF enjoyer Zach Wilson, whom we wish a speedy recovery).
  • Lastly, Zane: the only player in the league to honor the passing of Queen Elizabeth II put together a performance worthy of a knighthood, and his bench is looking formidable as well.
